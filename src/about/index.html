What is the FPA?

The Fairfield Programming Association is from Fairfield, Connecticut and is an open-source, 501(c)(3) organization. We run a number of programs to help educate high-school and college students- especially those who do not have access to programs.

Our Headquarters

The Fairfield Programming Association is headquartered at Greens Farms Academy in Westport. The campus was donated by the Vanderbilt family and the original mansion is still intact. Members of the Fairfield Programming Association will be able to tour the school and all of its beauty.
